Why Use Canva?

Feeling creatively stumped? dont have time to spend on learning a whole new software? Well Canva is here to save the day! Canva is a super easy design program ready to help unleash your creativity. With thousands of templates ready for you to help your small business, career, or personal life.

They have tutorials that go in depth with all their features as well as a helpful community that has solved so many creative problems.

Need your design turned into a product? Canva also has third party that will put your design on many items such as clothes, mugs, and calendars just to name a few.

Ready to get creative? Here is a link to check out Canva!

Pricing and Plans

Now you’re probably thinking that this must be expensive that there isn’t anyway all this is free…well it actually is! Canva has a free version of this program ready for you to get designing right now.

While you don’t have every feature available to you, it is a great way of getting your toes wet. If you decide to upgrade to the paid version, you get every feature, plus literally millions of more photos, elements, and audio sounds. The upgraded version is $12.95 a month or you can pay annually at $120 and save some money.

If you have a group of people you collaborate with then consider looking into a teams account.
