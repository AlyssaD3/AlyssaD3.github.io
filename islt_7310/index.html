I have selected to do an education website because I feel I have some self-taught knowledge of how to use Canva. I have used Canva faithfully for about three years now. I use it almost every day. There are so many great things that can be made from it. The software is incredibly easy to use as well as create a unique design in moments.

My goal for you

Even someone with zero graphic skills can design a flyer, resume, brochure, T-shirt quickly and easily. My goal is to break down how to make a design to someone who has never used Canva and show them how their creativity can take them down a new and exciting path.

Canva's free design library is growing, and their features are expanding. Learning about graphic design can be a scary thing to try, but with Canva's user friendly design software it can help access the creators untapped potential.

Why Use Canva?

Feeling creatively stumped? dont have time to spend on learning a whole new software? Well Canva is here to save the day! Canva is a super easy design program ready to help unleash your creativity. With thousands of templates ready for you to help your small business, career, or personal life.

They have tutorials that go in depth with all their features as well as a helpful community that has solved so many creative problems.

Need your design turned into a product? Canva also has third party that will put your design on many items such as clothes, mugs, and calendars just to name a few.
