Intro to Canva



Here is a quick video explaining how to use Canva. Do not be afraid, Canva is very user friendly and there is not a steep learning curve. You do not even need to feel "creatively inclined" as Canva will help you along the way. Signing up is free and even better, Canva has a lot of free features!

Need help with understanding the color wheel? Unsure of what colors are eye catching? This video will break down everything you need to know to tackle your next project!

What if you already have your company colors? Canva has them too! Simply type in the color hexcodes into your branding kit so they will always be available. It's even easier than you may think!
