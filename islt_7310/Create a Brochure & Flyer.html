Create a Brochure & Flyer





After you sign in you can start a quick search and typing in a keyword like "brochure". You will be instantly taken to thousands of free templates to use! There are filters off to the left side to help you narrow down what you are looing for. Or, you have the option for creating a new brochure from scratch! The ones to the left were made in a matter of minutes with using a template and a font and color scheme!

Need to add in photos? Canva has all different frame shapes to help you out. Simply click on a frame to add it into your project. Upload your photo. Hover your photo over the frame and the frame will automatically grab the photo and insert it! From there, you can resize the photo to your liking.

One of the best things about Canva is the resizing feature. Lets say you already make a flyer and now you need a brochure. Instead of recreating the whole design all over again you can click on "Resize". Type in the size in either inches, pixels, centimeters, or millimeters. Or you can type "brochure" (as an example) in the search bar and Canva will help you out with the appropriate size. This is a Premium Feature and not available with the free account.
